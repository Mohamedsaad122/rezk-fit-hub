# AI Platform Architecture

This document outlines the Enterprise AI architecture and intelligent coaching pipelines.

## Modules & Pipelines
* **Coach Assistant**: Synthesizes client historical activities and progress metrics.
* **Nutrition Assistant**: Calculates macros targets and outputs meal program recommendations.
* **Workout Generator**: Evaluates fitness goals to draft workout routines.
* **Insights Engine**: Computes drop-out risk assessments and mitigation alerts.
