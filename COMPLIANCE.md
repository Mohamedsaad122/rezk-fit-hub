# Regulatory Compliance & Auditing Framework

Rezk Fit Hub matches the governance requirements of SOC2, ISO 27001, and GDPR.

## Compliances Covered

### 1. SOC 2 Type II
- **Trust Service Criteria**: Security, Confidentiality, and Availability.
- **Controls**: AES-256 data-at-rest encryption, rate limiting, and access logs.

### 2. ISO 27001:2022
- **Information Security Management Systems (ISMS)**.
- **Controls**: Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), active session revocation, and security keys rotation.

### 3. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **Data Protection Controls**: Right to be forgotten (logs wiping), IP anonymization, and cookies consent.
