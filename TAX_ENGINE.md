# Tax Engine

This guide documents the tax calculations, VAT/GST rule structures, and country rules mapping.

## Rules Engine
* **Supported taxes**: VAT, GST, Sales Tax.
* **Country Resolution**: Maps country codes (e.g. SA to 15% VAT, AE to 5% VAT) during checkouts to calculate correct net tax amount.
* **Management Panel**: Configuration views allow adding or updating country tax rates.
